DISCOVER OUR STORY

Glory Restaurant began on April 26th, 2002 in the city of Hacienda Heights. Focusing on the goals of “Quality, Healthy, and Tradition” the company is family operation making each dish in our own kitchens from scratch. Glory Restaurant has three generations of family member working together to fulfill the idea “Honor the Customer, Quality Comes First”. Through hard work and dedication, Glory Restaurant began building its brand around the idea of delicious and authentic traditional Chinese food,Sea food,Italian Food.
